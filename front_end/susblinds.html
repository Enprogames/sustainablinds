SustainaBlinds

A Solar Heat Gain Calculator With an Interactive Interface
Dark Theme

About the site:

  • Automates your blinds to their optimal position

  • Lowers energy cost with efficient usage of sun energy

  • Calculates heat gain depending on various inputs

  • Can be altered at any time within the application

Heating and Cooling are the biggest factors in commercial energy consumption in Canada SustainaBlinds uses blind positioning to help reduce energy consumption. We aim to maximize solar gain when the building is calling for heat, and minimize it when calling for cooling.

With this interactive interface, we can recreate SustainaBlinds functionalities.
By setting the Time of Day, Month and Window Position, we can determine what the solar gain
is with the blinds open or when they are closed. From there we can determine the optimal position the blinds should be in.
