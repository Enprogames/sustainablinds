How we came up with SustainaBlinds:

  We were sitting in class one cold January morning, snow was on the ground yet the sun was shining. Despite the freezing temperature outside, we were forced to change into t-shirts in our classroom, that is when we realized the potential of solar gain heating.

How to use interactive diagram:

  • Press play to open interactive diagram

  • Use slider to adjust the time of day, from 4am - 8pm.

  • Select the month and the direction the window is facing using the select boxes.

  • Press the Calculate button to find how much heat gain the window is providing

  • Input outside temperature for suggested blind position.
